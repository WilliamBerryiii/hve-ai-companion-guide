## Key Takeaways

### 1. ADRs prevent knowledge loss in codebases

Without documented decisions, teams waste time asking "why did we do this?" Architecture knowledge disappears when engineers leave. ADRs preserve context, rationale, and alternatives for future maintainers.

### 2. AI streamlines ADR creation significantly

GitHub Copilot extracts context from research conversations and structures it into MADR format. AI assistance reduces the friction of documentation by generating consistent formats and prompting for comprehensive alternative documentation. The cognitive load of "blank page syndrome" disappears when AI provides a structured draft to refine.

### 3. ADRs integrate naturally into RPI phases

Research phase identifies alternatives. Planning phase documents decisions. Implementation phase references ADRs. Review phase verifies alignment. ADRs are not extra overhead. They are natural artifacts of deliberate engineering.

### 4. ADRs are living documents, not write-once artifacts

Good ADRs evolve with implementation learnings. Add notes when assumptions change. Supersede decisions when context shifts. Maintain ADR health through regular reviews and metrics.

### 5. Team ADR standards scale better than individual practices

Shared templates reduce decision fatigue. Approval workflows ensure alignment. Cross-references build navigable decision networks. Metrics reveal process health. Team standards turn ADRs from documentation into knowledge management.
